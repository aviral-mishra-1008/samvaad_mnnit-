Who are we?

We as GLASNOST 4R aim to create an atmosphere where collecting learning, coordination and mutual support helps us improve ourselves and put forward the outcomes of our hard work and cognitions. GLASNOST is a Russian word which means openess and this promotes the idea that is conveyed through our project and 4Râ€™s represent four nerds who are trying to become geeks. With that in mind we have recently created a website called SAMVAAD. SAMVAAD is an article site which provide a platform to MNNITians to interact, and put forward their views regarding events happening in our institution and share their experiences. It also includes special articles published by backend team which can work as the representation of MNNIT Allahabad to the external world. Our vision is to make Samvaad the go-to website for people searching about MNNIT. We got inspiration for this from Monday Morning Website made by the students of NIT Rourkela. Our website is created in an interactive and user friendly manner where students can post anything they feel and like to share supported by a backend moderation to remove disputes and conflicts. The moderation exists to avoid controversies and spams. This will help to keep the atmosphere of the website safe and peaceful. The submissions page is where people can submit articles and a Uinque Id is generated. A mail is sent to the backend team with user details and U_id. The backend team then logs in to the interactive website backend and reviews the article which if approved automatically produces an html file for the same. This file automatically gets rendered into an article and can be viewed or searched.
